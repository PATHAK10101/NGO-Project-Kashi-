About Us

Our Mission

We strive to provide quality education, healthcare, and sustainable development opportunities for the underprivileged.

Our Vision

Empowering individuals to achieve holistic growth, fostering an inclusive and sustainable society.

Our History

Founded in 1990, we have worked across communities to improve livelihoods and bring about sustainable change.

What We Do

At Kashinath Pathak Jan Swasth Evam Manovikash Seva Samiti, our mission is to uplift the underprivileged by providing access to quality education, essential healthcare, and sustainable development opportunities. We work tirelessly to ensure that every child, regardless of background, has the chance to succeed in life.

Bridge Course for Non-School Going Children

Providing education for children who have missed out on schooling.

Remedial Education

Helping children catch up and excel in their academic journey.

Scholarship Support

Providing financial assistance to deserving students.

Our Approach

At Kashinath Pathak Jan Swasth Evam Manovikash Seva Samiti, we believe in a holistic approach that emphasizes empowerment, sustainable partnerships, and innovative solutions. Our core pillars are based on uplifting underprivileged individuals and creating lasting impact.

Empowering Individuals

We prioritize capacity building and skills development to equip individuals with the tools they need for self-sufficiency.

Attracting Resilient Partnerships

We collaborate with organizations and communities to foster partnerships that drive innovation and resilience.

Innovative Sustainability

We integrate sustainable development practices into all of our initiatives to ensure long-term, positive change.
